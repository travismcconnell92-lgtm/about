Who We Are

Orlando's Small Business Law Firm

Ready Legal is a business law firm focused on construction law and real estate litigation. Centrally located in Orlando, it serves all of Central Florida. The firm was built with a singular mission: to provide cost-effective, personalized legal representation to the hardworking contractors, real estate professionals, and entrepreneurs who keep the community growing.

Foundation

Built From the Ground Up

Ready Legal was founded in Orlando in 2023 with a singular focus: providing small businesses with prompt, capable, and cost-effective legal representation. The firm draws on a decade of construction industry experience — from Army engineering to large-firm practice — to serve Florida contractors, suppliers, and small business owners with the same standard of representation that larger companies take for granted.

The Name

In the U.S. reserve armed forces, "readiness" refers to a unit's level of preparation and discipline — available when called upon. Ready Legal's name pays homage to this core value of prompt and effective service.

Solo Practice Advantage

As a solo practice, Ready Legal maintains a 0% employee turnover rate. Your matter is handled by the same attorney from start to finish — no handoffs, no gatekeepers.

Communication Promise

A common client complaint in the legal industry is lack of communication. When you call with a question, you will speak directly to your attorney.

Client Service

Clients First, Always

Ready Legal was born out of the idea that Orlando's small business community should have access to prompt, personalized, and cost-effective legal representation in their times of need. That core value is embodied in the firm's name.

Ready Legal offers small businesses a personalized alternative to standard law firm bureaucracy, providing each client with individualized and consistent representation. The firm prides itself on deep, longstanding attorney-client relationships — built on direct communication and consistent handling of every matter.

Ready Legal understands that time is valuable and a business is its owner's livelihood. That's why the firm prioritizes accessibility, responsiveness, and practical solutions that work in the real world.
